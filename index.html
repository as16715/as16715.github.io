ABOUT THE NYUAD CATS

The Infamous NYUAD cats call Saadiyat Island their home. These feline residents enjoy the comfort and care of our campus. You can often spot them lounging on plush chairs, basking in the sunlight. However, on those days when you crave a serotonin boost and can't find your beloved furry friend, fret not. I've put together a guide to our renowned campus cats and which side of the campus to look for them.

-Cat enthusiast, Aysha Salma
